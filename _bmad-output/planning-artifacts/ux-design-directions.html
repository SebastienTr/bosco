A — Classic Floating

White floating cards on the map. Clean, airy, Google Maps-inspired. The boat name card sits top-left, the ports button top-right, stats bar floats at the bottom. Maximum map visibility. Closest to the existing prototype's approach but refined.

B — Navy Bars

Dark navy bars top and bottom framing the map. Bold, nautical instrument feel. The map is framed like a porthole. Stats bar feels like a ship's instrument panel. More structured, more "maritime pro".

C — Sand & Warm

Sand-colored overlays for a warm, logbook feel. The boat card spans the full width at top with an integrated Share button. Stats labels in coral for warmth. Feels like a vintage nautical chart with modern UI. The warmest, most "adventure journal" direction.
